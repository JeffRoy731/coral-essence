LPS Corals

LPS (Large Polyp Stony) corals are known for their fleshy, flowing polyps and stunning colours. Perfect for intermediate reefers, they add movement and drama to any reef tank. These corals have larger, more visible polyps and generally require moderate lighting and flow.

SPS Corals

SPS (Small Polyp Stony) corals are the crown jewels of reef keeping. Known for their intricate branching structures and vibrant colours, they require stable water parameters and strong lighting. For experienced reefers looking to create stunning reef scapes.

Expert Care

The most diverse and sought-after SPS genus. From staghorn to table varieties, Acros display incredible colours and growth forms. The ultimate reef-building coral.

Soft Corals

Soft corals are the perfect starting point for new reef keepers. Without a hard calcium skeleton, these flexible beauties are forgiving, fast-growing, and come in an incredible array of colours. They add movement and life to any tank.
